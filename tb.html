The Brass

     

Art making can be pretty wasteful and sticking hams, racks of ribs, and steaks into alginate so you can make a meat barbell is probably a lot of people’s definition of waste. I crossed these images out because I wasn't quite sure what I was doing at the time or why I was doing it. I’m not sure I know now. I still appreciate the eccentric set of decisions that went into creating this work. I appreciate that is existed.
