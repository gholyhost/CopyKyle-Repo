Faking it With Family

     

A surreal representation of a family gathering personifying the personalities of absent individuals through the subtle differences in the objects they have left behind.

Hand blown glass mugs, ink on paper, digital newspaper print, bird seed, flower, wire, insulation foam, wood, fabric, and packing peanuts.
